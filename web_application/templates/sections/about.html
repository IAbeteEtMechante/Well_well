AI to serve

Our system is able to find the best locations of clean groundwater and evaluate the minerals/chemical composition of that water. Then depending on the GPS location of the device, our AI system will predict the best location

Hardware to answer

Our handheld device made with poor communities in mind, with no connectivity or secure access to power sources, we use ultra low power components, charged for many weeks within one day of sunlight, with a mission to guide the user to the nearest best well location, using 4 LEDs as guider arrows towards the right direction, and with the ability to change to the next-best location using a single button, it’s a 2” device with a microcomputer and a GPS sensor with lots of locations precalculated by the AI model, aimed to determine the current user location and guide them to the best-nearest location
